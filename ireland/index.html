No. We never charge application or registration fees. Our platform is completely free to join and use.

Payments are processed after tasks pass quality checks. Times may vary depending on method and region, but most workers receive payment within 24 hours of task completion. Everything is managed via WhatsApp.
What equipment do I need?
A smartphone and internet connection are sufficient for most tasks. Some roles may prefer a computer, but it's not necessary for most of our tasks.
How will you contact me?
We contact primarily via WhatsApp. You'll receive step-by-step instructions once you apply. All tasks and payments are managed through WhatsApp.
Do I need a WhatsApp account?
Yes, a WhatsApp account is required to receive tasks and manage payments. It's a secure, free platform we use to facilitate communication and the work process.
